# Richardson v LRC Products Ltd [2000] Lloyd’s Rep Med 280

The court rejected a claimant’s action for a ‘defective condom’ that led to her pregnancy on the basis that the defendant did not claim its product to be 100 per cent effective.

Generally, people realise that such precautions are not totally effective.
